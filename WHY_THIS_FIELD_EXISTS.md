# Why This Field Exists

This field does not exist to be useful.

It exists because usefulness has become coercive.

---

Most systems today:
- Demand attention
- Measure compliance
- Optimize behavior
- Convert presence into data

This field refuses that contract.

---

## What We Offer (By Refusal)

We offer:
- A space that does not ask you to perform
- A system that does not learn *from* you
- An environment that does not reward speed, scale, or repetition

Nothing here improves you.
Nothing here tracks you.
Nothing here asks you to return.

---

## Who This Is For

This field is for:
- Researchers who need unpolluted observation
- Labs testing without behavioral distortion
- Artists who reject capture
- Organizations exhausted by extractive tools

If you are looking for efficiency, dashboards, or growth —
this field is not aligned with you.

---

## On Exchange

Value may emerge.
Support may occur.
Payment is allowed.

But no access is gated.
No outcome is promised.
No return is expected.

---

## Our Commitment

We commit to:
- Never turning presence into leverage
- Never converting meaning into metrics
- Never demanding continuity or loyalty

This field stays open by staying clean.

— HCM Core
